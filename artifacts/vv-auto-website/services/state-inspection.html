Texas Annual Vehicle Inspection

Texas law requires all registered vehicles to pass an annual safety and emissions inspection. The inspection verifies that your vehicle's safety systems are working properly and that your vehicle meets Texas emissions standards.

At V.V. Auto, we make the process fast and painless. If your vehicle fails inspection, we'll tell you exactly what needs to be fixed and give you a fair price to correct it. In many cases, we can do the repairs and get you re-inspected the same day.

What Gets Inspected

  • Brakes and brake lights
  • Steering and suspension
  • Tires and wheels
  • Headlights, taillights, turn signals

Also Checked

  • Horn
  • Windshield wipers
  • OBD emissions scan (newer vehicles)
  • Window tint compliance
