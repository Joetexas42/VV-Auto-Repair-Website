About V.V. Auto

A family-owned mechanic shop built on trust, fair pricing, and reliable work in Dallas and Garland.

Our Story

V.V. Auto Repair was founded with a straightforward mission: to give every car owner in Dallas and Garland an auto shop they can genuinely rely on. For too long, many drivers felt like they couldn't trust their mechanic — that they'd be overcharged, rushed through, or talked into repairs they didn't need. We set out to change that.

As a Vietnamese-American family business, we serve our community in both English and Tiếng Việt. We understand that language barriers can make it harder to get the service you need — so we make sure every customer, regardless of background, receives clear explanations, fair estimates, and respectful service.

Our Promise

"We never recommend a repair unless we genuinely believe you need it. We'll show you what we find, explain what it means, and let you make the call. That's what honest service looks like."

Today, we operate two specialized locations: our Dallas shop handles mechanical repairs — from oil changes and brake jobs to engine diagnostics and full rebuilds. Our Garland location is a dedicated collision and body shop, handling everything from minor dents to major accident damage and insurance claims.

With over 65 Google reviews averaging 4.4 stars, we're proud of the reputation we've built — one honest repair at a time.

Our Values

Transparency

We show you exactly what we find and explain every recommendation in plain language before doing any work.

Quality Work

We use quality parts and take the time to do repairs right. We stand behind our work.

Community

We're part of the Dallas/Garland community and serve our neighbors with the same respect we'd want for ourselves.

Family Values

We treat every customer the way we'd treat a member of our own family — with care, honesty, and respect.
